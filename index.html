Welcome to my Lord of the Rings Website

This website is dedicated to the characters of The Lord of the Rings, with a special focus on the nine members of the Fellowship and the complex figure of Gollum. Here you'll find thoughtful character profiles that explore each individual’s role, strengths, and personality — from Frodo’s quiet resolve to Aragorn’s leadership, from Gandalf’s wisdom to Sam’s unwavering loyalty.

Whether you're revisiting the story or discovering new details along the way, this site offers a place to appreciate the people behind the quest — not just as heroes of legend, but as characters with flaws, growth, and heart.

Thanks for visiting, and enjoy your journey through the Fellowship.
